Fiverr Assistant

Automate button clicks on Fiverr freelancer pages

How to Use

  1. Navigate to a Fiverr freelancer page at pro.fiverr.com/freelancers/*
  2. Press Enter to click the Message button
  3. Press Space to automatically click buttons in sequence:
